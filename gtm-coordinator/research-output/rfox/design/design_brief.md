# Design Brief: rFOX Program

## Concept
Announcement graphic for the rFOX program launch on ShapeShift.

## Dimensions Needed
- Twitter: 1200x675
- Square (Discord/Instagram): 1080x1080
- Blog header: 1600x900

## Brand Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #3761F9 (ShapeShift blue)
- Background: #1a1a2e (dark)
- Accent: #00D395 (green for positive)
- Text: #FFFFFF

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body: Space Grotesk

**Logo:**
- ShapeShift fox in corner (not center)
- rFOX logo can be centered or prominent
- Never distort logos

## Visual Direction

Clean, minimalist, professional DeFi aesthetic.
- No gradients
- No 3D effects
- No stock photos
- High contrast
