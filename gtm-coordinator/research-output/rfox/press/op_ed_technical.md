### How rFOX Works

rFOX is a staking program on Arbitrum ratified by FOX token holders (SCP-166; SCP-186 moved to USDC distribution in February 2026). Users stake FOX (after bridging to Arbitrum in the ShapeShift UI if on mainnet); the contract tracks staked balances and reward units. Stakers receive a percentage of the ShapeShift DAO's total revenue from **affiliate fees** each epoch. Distribution is **off-chain by the DAO multisig**, as USDC to the staker's wallet—sent on the **15th of each month** for the preceding month. When users unstake, they stop earning and enter a **28-day cooldown** before they can claim their FOX back. Accounting is published to IPFS and the forum each epoch; the open source CLI in the [rFOX repo](https://github.com/shapeshift/rFOX) lets the community verify amounts. No custody, no KYC.

### What the rFOX Program Enables Technically

- **Self-custodial revenue share:** Stakers keep control of keys; rewards are distributed off-chain to their wallets.
- **Transparent accounting:** On-chain reward units plus public IPFS/forum records and verifiable CLI align with open-source and auditability.
- **Arbitrum deployment:** Single-sided FOX staking on Arbitrum with configurable cooldown and upgradeable contracts (DAO-owned).
