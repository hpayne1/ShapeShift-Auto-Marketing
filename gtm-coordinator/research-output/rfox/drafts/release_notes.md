# rFOX Program

rFOX is now live: stake FOX on Arbitrum, earn USDC—rewards in your wallet on the 15th of each month.

## What's New

- Stake FOX on Arbitrum (bridge in ShapeShift UI if on mainnet), earn USDC. Rewards sent on the 15th of each month for the preceding month.
- Rewards from a share of the DAO's affiliate-fee revenue each epoch; off-chain distribution by DAO multisig.
- 28-day cooldown when unstaking before FOX can be claimed.
- No custody, no KYC—your keys, your control. Accounting on IPFS and forum each epoch; verify with the [rFOX repo](https://github.com/shapeshift/rFOX) CLI.
