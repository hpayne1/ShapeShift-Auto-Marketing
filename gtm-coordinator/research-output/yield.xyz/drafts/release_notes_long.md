# Release notes (long): Yield.xyz

**Release Notes: ShapeShift x Yield.xyz Integration**

ShapeShift is thrilled to announce the integration of Yield.xyz, a leading Web3 yield infrastructure platform, into our ecosystem. This integration enhances your ability to seamlessly access decentralized finance tools and maximize returns on your crypto assets. Below are the details of this exciting update:

### What's New:
- **Comprehensive Yield Farming Solutions**: Users can now engage in advanced yield farming strategies directly through ShapeShift, leveraging Yield.xyz’s robust infrastructure.
- **Staking Services**: Securely stake your assets to earn competitive rewards, backed by Yield.xyz's secure staking mechanisms.
- **Access to DeFi Data APIs**: Gain insights with real-time data analytics and make informed decisions using Yield.xyz’s scalable API infrastructure.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Experience an intuitive and seamless interaction with Yield.xyz’s cross-platform compatible tools.

### How to Use It:
1. **Access Yield Farming**: Log into your ShapeShift account and navigate to the Yield section. Select Yield.xyz to explore available farming opportunities.
2. **Staking**: Choose the assets you wish to stake via the ShapeShift platform and follow the prompts to initiate staking through Yield.xyz.
3. **DeFi Data APIs**: Developers can integrate Yield.xyz’s data APIs by accessing ShapeShift’s developer portal and following the documentation provided.
4. **Cross-Promotional Activities**: Stay tuned for upcoming collaborative initiatives between ShapeShift and Yield.xyz that could enhance your yield opportunities.

### Technical Details:
- **Advanced Yield Optimization Algorithms**: Yield.xyz employs sophisticated algorithms to optimize yield farming strategies, maximizing your potential returns.
- **Real-Time Data Analytics**: Utilize Yield.xyz's real-time analytics to track market trends and performance metrics.
- **Secure Staking Mechanisms**: Benefit from Yield.xyz’s state-of-the-art security protocols ensuring the safety of your staked assets.

- **Smart Contract Vulnerabilities**: As with all DeFi platforms, there is an inherent risk of smart contract vulnerabilities. Users are encouraged to stay informed about security updates.
