# Wild Card Ideas

*Scrappy, low-budget, high-impact marketing ideas.*

---

## 1. Yield Meme Bounty Hunt

Launch a meme creation contest encouraging participants to create and share memes promoting Yield.xyz, with a bounty for the most viral meme.

- **Budget:** $500
- **Time:** 1 week
- **Target:** Crypto enthusiasts

**How to Execute:**
Announce the meme contest on social media and crypto forums.,Provide guidelines: memes must include Yield.xyz branding.,Set a submission deadline and reward for the most liked/shared meme.,Review submissions and announce winners, sending rewards in crypto.

---

## 2. DeFi Developer Challenge

Host a challenge for developers to integrate Yield.xyz into their existing projects, with rewards for the best integrations.

- **Budget:** $1,000
- **Time:** 2 weeks
- **Target:** Developers

**How to Execute:**
Announce the challenge on developer-centric platforms like GitHub and Reddit.,Provide resources and documentation for Yield.xyz integration.,Mark deliverables and reward criteria, e.g., creativity, usability.,Review submissions, select winners, and award prizes in crypto.

## 3. Reply-Guy Yield Campaign

Run a campaign where enthusiastic community members engage with crypto influencer posts, subtly incorporating Yield.xyz into conversations.

- **Budget:** $200
- **Time:** Ongoing (as needed)
- **Target:** DeFi investors

**How to Execute:**
Recruit enthusiastic community members by offering them crypto tips for participation.,Identify key influencers in the DeFi space and monitor their posts.,Create engaging and informative replies that naturally mention or endorse Yield.xyz features.,Encourage community to upvote and engage to increase visibility.

## 4. Yield Prediction Market Surprise

Create a pseudo-prediction market that rewards accurate predictions about DeFi trends using Yield.xyz tokens.

- **Budget:** $800
- **Time:** 1 month
- **Target:** Institutional investors

**How to Execute:**
Utilize an existing prediction market platform to set up events related to DeFi trends.,Announce the competition and encourage participation using social media and direct outreach.,Set a timeline for predictions and define criteria for accuracy.,Award participants who make the most accurate predictions with Yield.xyz tokens.

---

## 5. Flash Trading Contest

Organize a time-limited trading contest on Yield.xyz with rewards for the most trades executed in a short timeframe.

- **Budget:** $1,000
- **Time:** 1 day
- **Target:** DeFi investors, Crypto enthusiasts

**How to Execute:**
Announce the contest rules and timeframe on social media and through existing Yield.xyz communication channels.,Encourage participants to register and participate in the trading window.,Track trading activities and declare winners based on the number of trades and profitability.,Distribute rewards promptly to winners, creating an engaging follow-up communication.
