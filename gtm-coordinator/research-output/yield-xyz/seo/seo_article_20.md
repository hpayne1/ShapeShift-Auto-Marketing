## What is DeFi Yield and Why Self-Custody Matters?

DeFi yield refers to the returns earned from various decentralized finance activities, such as staking, lending, and participating in liquidity pools. Unlike traditional finance, DeFi enables users to earn yield directly from their crypto assets without intermediaries.

Self-custody is the practice of holding and managing one's own private keys, which are essential for accessing and controlling digital assets. This approach ensures that users maintain complete control over their funds, protecting them from third-party risks associated with centralized exchanges or custodial services.
