## Understanding CEX Custody Risks

Centralized exchanges serve as intermediaries, holding users' assets in their wallets. While convenient, this model poses significant risks:

- **Loss of Control**: Users must trust the exchange to manage their assets securely.
- **Security Breaches**: CEXs are prime targets for hackers due to the vast amounts of assets held.
- **Regulatory Risks**: Changes in regulations can affect access to funds or services.

To mitigate these risks, many crypto enthusiasts are turning to DeFi solutions that prioritize self-custody.

## How DeFi Ensures Self-Custody

Decentralized finance platforms empower users by eliminating the need for intermediaries. With DeFi, individuals maintain control over their private keys, ensuring that only they can access their assets. This self-sovereign approach aligns with the vision of a borderless financial system that is open and in your hands.
