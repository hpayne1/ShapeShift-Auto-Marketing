## Understanding Aave and ShapeShift

Before diving into how to use Aave through ShapeShift, it's essential to understand what these platforms are and how they function within the DeFi ecosystem.

### What is Aave?

Aave is a decentralized lending protocol that allows users to lend and borrow cryptocurrencies without intermediaries. It operates on Ethereum and other blockchain networks, providing a variety of assets for borrowing and lending. Users can earn interest on deposits and use their assets as collateral for loans.

### What is ShapeShift?

ShapeShift is a decentralized platform that facilitates self-custody and privacy in cryptocurrency transactions. It offers a user-friendly interface to access a range of DeFi protocols, including Aave, without the need for KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance. ShapeShift's mission is to empower individuals with direct access to open protocols and decentralized financial tools.

## How Aave Works: A General Overview

Aave operates by pooling funds from depositors, which are then made available for borrowers. Here's a simplified breakdown of how it functions:

- **Depositors:** Provide liquidity to the Aave protocol by depositing assets into the pool. In return, they earn interest over time.
- **Borrowers:** Access funds from the pool by providing collateral. Borrowers pay interest on the borrowed amount, which is distributed to depositors.
- **Aave Tokens (aTokens):** When you deposit assets, you receive aTokens, which represent your stake and accrue interest over time.
