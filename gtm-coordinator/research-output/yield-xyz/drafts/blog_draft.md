# ShapeShift Welcomes Yield.xyz: Expanded Horizons for DeFi Power Users

## What if you could access diverse yield opportunities without leaving ShapeShift?

ShapeShift now offers the integration of Yield.xyz, a robust infrastructure designed for those who seek to maximize their yield potential across multiple blockchain networks. This development empowers ShapeShift users to stake, earn, and access a wide range of DeFi opportunities without compromising on the principles of self-custody and privacy.

### What is Yield.xyz?

Yield.xyz stands as the ultimate Web3 yield infrastructure, providing a unified API for accessing yield across more than 75 networks. Whether you're looking to stake, earn from stablecoin yields, or dive into deeper DeFi opportunities, Yield.xyz simplifies the process. It offers a comprehensive and streamlined approach to managing yield, aiming to enhance users' revenue potential by up to 2.8 times with minimal complexity involved.

### What Can You Do Now?

With the integration of Yield.xyz on ShapeShift, users get a suite of opportunities:

- **Unified Access**: Connect with a single API to explore yield options across 75+ networks.
- **Diverse Opportunities**: Access staking, stablecoin yields, and a variety of DeFi services all in one place.
- **Enhanced Revenue Potential**: Maximize returns with the potential for up to 2.8x higher revenue compared to traditional methods.

This integration makes it easier than ever for users to manage and optimize their crypto assets, leveraging the full spectrum of yield opportunities available in the decentralized finance world.

### Why This Matters

At the core of ShapeShift's mission lies the principle of self-custody. Users maintain control over their assets without the need for intermediaries or relinquishing their keys. This integration with Yield.xyz aligns perfectly with our commitment to privacy and decentralization, as ShapeShift continues to operate with no KYC requirements. Users can explore these yield opportunities securely and privately, embodying the true spirit of decentralized finance.

### How to Get Started

Getting started with Yield.xyz on ShapeShift is straightforward. Follow these steps to begin your yield journey:

1. **Log into ShapeShift**: Access your ShapeShift account or create one if you're new.
2. **Navigate to Yield Options**: Find the Yield.xyz integration under the DeFi section of the platform.
3. **Explore Opportunities**: Browse through the available yield options across multiple networks.
4. **Select and Stake**: Choose the yield opportunity that suits your strategy and proceed to stake your assets.
5. **Monitor Your Earnings**: Keep track of your yield performance directly within the ShapeShift interface.
