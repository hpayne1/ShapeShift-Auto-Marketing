## User Problems We're Solving

1. **KYC gatekeeping.** Most places to buy or swap into ZEC require identity verification. Users who want ZEC for its privacy properties find this self-defeating.
2. **Privacy coin delistings.** Centralized exchanges are dropping ZEC and other privacy coins. Users need alternatives to acquire and hold ZEC.
3. **Single-chain wallet limitations.** Zcash-specific wallets (Zashi, YWallet) don't support BTC, ETH, or other assets. Users end up managing multiple wallets.
4. **UTXO + EVM fragmentation.** Managing UTXO coins (ZEC, BTC) alongside EVM tokens requires different tools. Users want one interface.
5. **No direct swap path.** Users holding BTC or ETH who want ZEC typically have to go through a centralized exchange. They need a direct swap.
6. **Custody risk on exchanges.** Users who buy ZEC on a CEX don't hold their own keys until they withdraw â€” and some CEXs restrict ZEC withdrawals.
