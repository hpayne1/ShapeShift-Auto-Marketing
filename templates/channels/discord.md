# Discord Announcement Template
## For #announcements or #general channels

---

## Discord vs X: Key Differences

| Aspect | X | Discord |
|--------|---|---------|
| Audience | Public, discovery | Community, engaged |
| Tone | Punchy, viral | Warmer, detailed |
| Length | 280 chars | Up to 2000 chars |
| Formatting | Limited | Rich (bold, links, embeds) |
| Engagement | Likes, RTs | Reactions, replies |

**Discord is for your community.** They already know ShapeShift. You can be more detailed, more personal, and assume more context.

---

## Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Friendly, informative, community-first. Like talking to friends who care about the product.

**Formatting to use:**
- **Bold** for emphasis
- `code blocks` for technical terms
- Bullet points for lists
- Emojis for visual breaks (but don't overdo it)
- Line breaks for readability

**Community language:**
- "foxes" â€” ShapeShift community members (use sparingly)
- "we" and "our" â€” inclusive language
- Address the reader directly: "you can now..."

## Character Limits

- Discord message: 2000 characters
- Embed title: 256 characters
- Embed description: 4096 characters
- Aim for: 800-1200 chars for readability

---

## Quality Checklist

Before posting:

- [ ] Clear headline with emoji
- [ ] One main message/announcement
- [ ] Formatted for easy scanning (bullets, bold)
- [ ] Links work
- [ ] CTA is clear (what should they do?)
- [ ] Tone is warm and community-appropriate
- [ ] Proofread for typos
- [ ] Correct channel selected
